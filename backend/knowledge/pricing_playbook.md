# Pricing & Renewal Playbook

## Handling pricing objections
When a customer raises pricing concerns before renewal:
1. Reframe the conversation around value and ROI, not list price.
2. Quantify time saved, incidents reduced, and revenue protected.
3. Offer a clear comparison of Standard vs Enterprise tiers.
4. Bring an ROI summary to every pricing discussion.

## Renewal checklist
- Confirm the renewal date and notice period.
- Identify the economic buyer and the approval chain.
- Resolve every open support issue before the renewal date.
- Send a written renewal proposal at least 30 days in advance.
- Log the renewal risk level and mitigation plan.

## Pricing objection -> recommended action
A pricing objection within 30 days of renewal is a HIGH risk signal and should
trigger a pricing alignment meeting with an ROI summary.
