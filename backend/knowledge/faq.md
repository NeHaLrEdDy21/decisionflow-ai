# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Which plan includes SSO?**
A: Single sign-on (SAML) is included in the Enterprise tier.

**Q: How long does SSO take to set up?**
A: Usually 1-2 weeks depending on the identity provider.

**Q: Can we get a price comparison between tiers?**
A: Yes - your CSM can prepare a Standard vs Enterprise comparison and ROI summary.

**Q: What add-ons are available?**
A: Premium support (faster SLAs), admin training workshops, and advanced security.
