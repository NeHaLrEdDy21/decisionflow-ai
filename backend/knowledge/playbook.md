# Customer Success Playbook

## At-risk renewal (within 30 days)
Trigger proactive outreach. Combine a pricing/value conversation with
resolution of any open technical blockers (e.g., SSO).

## Expansion motions
- Upsell to Enterprise when security/SSO requirements appear.
- Cross-sell premium support when high-priority tickets are frequent.
- Offer training workshops to lift adoption and health scores.

## Stakeholder mapping
Always confirm the economic buyer before a renewal. A missing decision maker
is a top cause of slipped renewals.
